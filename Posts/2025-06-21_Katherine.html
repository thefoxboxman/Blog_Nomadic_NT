Katherine

Our next sightseeing stop is Katherine, 700km north of Tennant Creek. This means two days driving with little or no sightseeing. Not that there is much to see. Just wide open plains, scruby plains, low, red, rocky ranges and sparse towns. We left the cool centre and arrived two days later in the hot humid tropics. And this is winter up here. We stopped overnight at Daly Waters and were forced to change from flannelette sheets to bamboo.

Up very early for a sunrise cruise of the Nitmiluk Gorge (Katherin Gorge).
